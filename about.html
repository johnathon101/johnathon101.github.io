About Me


I began my coding career in January of 2014. I had a worked in home improvement for seven years previously. I enjoy the problem solving aspect of both careers equally. Coding gives me the opportunity to be more creative and build things more fun to interact with. I started Omaha Code School on February 23 and dedicated the next three months to training my processes and skills to build things for the web. I left Code School confident I could build solid web applications. I worked on a few freelance projects before taking an internship at Ervin & Smith. They have a web development department and a focus on marketing. I knew previously I could make a tested, complex and efficient logic for an applivcation so being able to delve into SEO and working with talented creative designers helped me gain appreciation for style and clean user interfaces.


If you have a need for web development or to chat about my experience get in touch!
